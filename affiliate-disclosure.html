Affiliate Disclosure

How we earn, how we review, and why your trust matters most.

Our Commitment to Transparency

At SparkNest, transparency is important to us. We believe you deserve to know exactly how we operate, how we make money, and how that affects the recommendations you read.

SparkNest is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program and other affiliate programs. We earn commissions from qualifying purchases at no additional cost to you.

What Are Affiliate Links?

Some of the links on this website are affiliate links. This means that if you click a link and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you.

These commissions help support the operation of this website, including research, content creation, and the development of free educational resources for families.

Does This Affect Our Reviews?

No. We strive to provide honest, balanced reviews and recommendations. Affiliate relationships do not influence our opinions, rankings, or conclusions.

Every product we recommend is tested by our team or thoroughly researched using real parent feedback. We reject affiliate partnerships with products that don't meet our standards — even if they pay well.

How We Choose Partners

We only partner with companies and products that:

  • Have strong parent reviews and proven educational value
  • Offer fair pricing and transparent business practices
  • Provide genuine value to children and families
  • Allow us to maintain editorial independence

Questions?

If you have questions about our affiliate relationships, please contact us. We're happy to explain our process in detail.
